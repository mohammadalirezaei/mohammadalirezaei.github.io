Research focus

I’m a Computer Vision Research Engineer at Part AI Research Center, where I build and ship generative-AI systems based on diffusion models and Diffusion Transformers. My interests sit at the intersection of text and image processing and building intelligent, agentic pipelines that turn research-grade models into dependable products. In parallel, my academic work centers on pedestrian trajectory and intent prediction for autonomous vehicles, combining computer vision with graph neural networks. Across both, the throughline is the same: make models fast, safe, and reliable enough to run at scale.
